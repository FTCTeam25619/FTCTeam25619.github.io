Our Team

In Memoriam

We are dedicating our season to one of our founding teammates,

Jeremiah Bivins

Jeremiah was a gifted and driven student focused on becoming a Civil Engineer. His passion for robotics was clear from the moment he began learning to code. What we remember most is his quiet humor and determination to succeed.

Our robot, Bullfrog, is named in his honor, inspired by the song “Joy to the World” by Three Dog Night.
