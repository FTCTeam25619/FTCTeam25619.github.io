Want to sponsor us?

We are thankful for all the support we get! Your generous donations provide our student team with invaluable real-world experience, allowing them to apply their knowledge and creativity in designing and building a robot.

We welcome donations of funds in any amount to cover our costs including:

  • robotics kit and supplemental parts
  • competition registration fees and transportation
  • tools and pit supplies
  • team shirts and spirit gear
  • updates to our equipment

To donate, please complete the FTC Team 25619 Sponsorship Form and return it by email or postal mail.

Donate online

Donate by mail

If you prefer to donate by mail, please enclose a check with your sponsorship form. Checks should be made payable to our fiscal sponsor: Hack Club.

In-kind donations

For donations of tools or equipment, such as wrenches, screwdrivers, hand power tools, or toolboxes, please include a list of donated items, as well as receipts and places of purchase.

If you are interested in donating a larger automated or power tool, such as a 3D printer, CNC machine, lathe, mill, or waterjet cutter, we would love to discuss it with you!
