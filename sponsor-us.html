Want to sponsor us?

We are thankful for all the support we get!

Your generous donations provide our student team with invaluable real-world experience, allowing them to apply their knowledge and creativity in designing and building a robot.

How to donate

To donate, please complete the FTC Team 25619 Sponsorship Form and return it by mail.

  • Checks should be made payable to Mandeville Junior High School.
  • For donations of tools or equipment, please include a receipt and place of purchase.

Donations we need

We welcome donations of funds in any amount to cover our costs including:

  • robotics kit and supplemental parts
  • competition registration fees
  • transportation
  • tools and pit supplies
  • team shirts and spirit gear
  • updates to our equipment

We also accept donations of tools and equipment, such as wrenches, screwdrivers, and toolboxes.
