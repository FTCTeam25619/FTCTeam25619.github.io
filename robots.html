Our Robots

Current Season:

We are currently competing in the DECODE℠ season presented by RTX© competition season. Find out information about our successes in this season so far here.

Our robot's name is Drakos, the modern Greek word for "serpent", derived from the ancient Greek word "drakon", which means "the watchful one." Our robot has a serpentine shape and a Limelight vision camera, functioning as a "watchful eye," and making the name a perfect fit.

Our robot consists of a hooded shooter with an adjustable angle and swyft modular custom drivetrain, which allows for versatile movement.

We would not have been able to achieve all we have achieved thus far without our sponsors, which you can find information about here.

We are excited for a fun and successful DECODE℠ season.

More information will be available as the season progresses.
