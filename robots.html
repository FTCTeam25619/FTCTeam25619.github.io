Our Robots

2024-2025

We competed in the INTO THE DEEP℠ presented by RTX© competition season and won various awards, seen here.

Our goals for this robot were to be able to score samples and specimens efficiently, succeed in performing a 2nd-level climb conststently, and have an overall fast and efficient robot, capable of conststently winning matches with exceptionally high scores.

Our robot had a holonomic drivetrain ideal for required tasks and maneuverability. The mecanum wheels are able to drive forwards and backwards, strafe, and drive diagonally, making them very versatile and maneuverable. We bought a kit for our drivetrain for simplicity purposes and because this was our rookie year.

Our robot's intake included two parts, two wheels and the grucket. The wheels spun to take in pieces to pass to the grucket. The grucket, a combination of the words grip and bucket, was a bucket with a grip to grab and keep grip of pieces recieved by the two-wheel intake.

We also added other features, such as 3d-printed hooks to allow us to climb, wheel covers to protect the wheels from getting damaged, lights to signal the drivers of when a piece was taken into the intake, and color sensors to tell what color pieces were entering the intake.
