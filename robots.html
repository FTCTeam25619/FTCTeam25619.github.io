Our Robots

Current Season:

We are currently competing in the DECODE℠ season presented by RTX© competition season. Our robot, when finished, will have a hooded shooter and a swyft modular custom drivetrain.

Possible names for our robot include the Serpent and the Snake, as we want to continue with our reptillian/amphibian theme, and snakes are often associated with archeology and ancient heiroglyphics.

More information will be available as the season progresses.

We are excited for a fun and successful DECODE℠ season.
